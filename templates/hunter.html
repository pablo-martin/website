Hunter S. Thompson Bot

Ever since I saw the AI-generated Harry Potter chapter, I wanted to see it for Hunter S. Thompson. A few came out, but none from the doctor, so I decided to do it myself. I trained a "deep" Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) neural network on the oeuvre of Hunter S. Thompson. After several days, it came to generate something reminiscent of the inventor of Gonzo journalism. You can check it below.



On the first box, you can specify how many characters you want to generate (max 200). On the second field, you can also specify the "temperature". The higher the temperature, the more random (funny) the text, but with a greater chance of typos and nonsense. The lower the temperature, the more "conservative" the guess. A good value is anything below 1. Type a phrase, like "the meaning of life is ", or "my television wants to " and click 'Autocomplete'. Or just click 'Generate New Phrase' to generate a random sentence. It does take a few seconds to process.
