Some Books I've Read

Around the summer of 2016, I saw my bookcase and realized I hadn't actually read ~70% of my books. I had read some, started others, and hadn't even opened most of them. I decided I wouldn't buy another book until I had read all the ones I already owned. I managed to do that, and then kept going. Here is a list of those books:

I found that reading, like anything, gets better with practice. It becomes easier to focus for a long time, to remember more details about what you're reading, and to have the patience to finish a longer book. It's a habit I'm happy I acquired, and now I don't dread a commute, I just see it as reading time.
